Children's Home Ministry

At present, there are 123 children living in the Thomas Children’s Home for the Needy. Some are orphans, others are from homes that cannot afford to meet their needs, and about 40% are pastors’ children from areas where they are persecuted for their faith in Jesus. In the home, the children are supplied with food, clothing, shelter, Christian love and guidance, and an education they would otherwise not be able to receive. The monthly cost for one child to stay in the home is $120, which is obtained from supporters of the ministry.

The property for the Thomas Children’s Home for the Needy is being leased, and the owner has indicated a desire to eventually sell the land. Because of this, Touch India Ministries is raising funds to build their own children’s home that can house 200 children. Proceeds from the Knees 2 War events support this building effort.

School Ministry

Currently, Touch India Ministries owns and operates the Thomas Memorial English School, offering an education for students from Pre-K to 10th grade. The school is located near Bangalore, India, and can hold up to 1,000 students. It is a Christian school and provides students with a much desired English education. Plans are to complete the school building by adding a fourth floor to house a two-year university prep school. Proceeds from the Knees 2 War events support this building effort.

Pastors Ministry

About 1600 pastors in the state of Karnataka are associated directly or indirectly with Touch India Ministries. Biblical training is provided for these pastors through seminars conducted by volunteer pastors from the USA. Supporters of the ministry also donate funds to supplement the pastors in areas where there is a need. Plans are to use part of the fourth floor of the school building for biblical training which will enable certificates to be issued to the pastors. This is a requirement by the government to be recognized as a legitimate pastor.

Widows Ministry

In India, there are thousands of widows, and the culture places little value on their lives. Many live on the streets and in dire poverty. A special ministry is to gift them with sarees, typical Indian dresses. Each year, Touch India Ministries is able to distribute hundreds of sarees as well as give them the message of hope and truth found only in Jesus Christ.

HIV Patient Ministry

HIV patients are put in AIDS colonies, and virtually left to die. Touch India Ministries encourages churches to support local pastors in these areas so they are able to reach out to the needs of these hurting people. While many neglect these precious souls, they are loved and hear the gospel message by these pastors.
